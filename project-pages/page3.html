Univeristy Project - Health Justice for Non Native Speakers in the UK
This project was hosted at the University of York as part of my academic degree, this involved prototyping and reasearching on a common issue within health and justice. For our project we decided to focus on the development of a mobile application that would provide a translation service for non-native speakers in the UK with a website and interactive AI powered chatbot.
