About Me
I am a current Doctoral Researcher in Safe Ethical AI at the University of York's Institute for Safe Autonomy. My work focuses on combining Digital Accessibility and Machine Learning techniques with a focus on creating sign language recognition models for use in real-life safety scenarios.
My interests and experiences span machine learning, interactive systems, and safe & ethical cyber security. In my spare time, I work as a commission-based artist and have won several awards for my work. Scroll down to learn more about me and my work!
