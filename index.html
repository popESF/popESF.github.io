About Me
I am a current Doctoral Researcher in Safe Ethical AI at the University of York's Institute for Safe Autonomy. My work focuses on combining Digital Accessibility and Machine learning techniques with a focus on creating sign language recognition models for use in real life safety scenarios. My interests and experiences stem across machine learning, interactive systems and safe & ethical cyber security. In my spare time i work as a commission based artist and have won several awards for my work. Scroll down to learn more about me and my work!
