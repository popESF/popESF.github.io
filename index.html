About

I am a Doctoral Researcher in Computer Science working on accessible, interactive technologies that combine computer vision and machine learning to support sign language recognition in realistic environments. My work spans applied ML, user-centred design for accessibility, and developing robust interactive prototypes for education and healthcare.
